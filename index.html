1. High-Protein Soya Masala Rice

Uses **50g** Soya Chunks + Free Rice. High satiety for the afternoon walk.

Method Highlights:

  • Soak, squeeze, and pan-fry Soya Chunks until slightly browned.
  • Prepare onion/tomato/spices curry base using free oil.
  • Mix the Soya Chunks with the curry and pre-cooked Free Rice.
Watch Quick Recipe Tutorial (Hindi)

2. Double-Protein Dal & Egg Curry

Uses **2 Eggs** + **100g** Dal. High protein for night muscle recovery.

Method Highlights:

  • Boil the **2 Eggs** and set aside.
  • Prepare a thick curry base, then add pre-cooked Dal and simmer.
  • Add peeled eggs to the curry. Serve hot with Free Rotis.
